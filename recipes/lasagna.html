Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with a rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted cheese, then baking it until golden and bubbly. It is hearty, flavorful, and perfect for family meals.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Marinara or tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese or béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella and Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the Meat Sauce: Brown ground beef in a pan, add marinara sauce, and simmer until thick.
  2. Prepare the Pasta: Cook lasagna sheets according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. Assemble Layers: In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, pasta sheets, ricotta or béchamel, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  4. Top and Bake: Finish with a layer of cheese and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  5. Rest and Serve: Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before cutting and serving.

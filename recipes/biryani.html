Biryani Recipe

Description

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic South Asian dish made with spiced rice, marinated meat (such as chicken, mutton, beef, or fish), and sometimes vegetables. It is cooked using a layering technique, where rice and meat are alternately stacked and slow-cooked with fragrant spices like saffron, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Meat
  • Masalas
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Marinate the Meat: Mix meat with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices (turmeric, chili powder, garam masala). Let it rest for 1-2 hours.
  2. Cook the Rice: Parboil basmati rice with whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cinnamon) until 70% cooked.
  3. Fry Onions: Deep fry thinly sliced onions until golden brown and crisp.
  4. Layer and Assemble: In a pot, layer the marinated meat and parboiled rice alternately. Add fried onions, fresh herbs (mint, coriander), and saffron milk on top.
  5. Dum Cook: Seal the pot with a tight lid or dough. Cook on low heat for 30-40 minutes until the meat is tender and the flavors blend.
